Macro Trends & Industry Shifts

Advanced Founders Program - Sprint 1: Think

Learning Objective: Understand how emerging technologies reshape industries and identify new business opportunities.

1. How Emerging Technologies (AI, Blockchain, Climate Tech) Reshape Industries

As advanced founders, staying ahead of macro trends and industry shifts is essential for the long-term success of your business. Global forces such as emerging technologies, policy changes, and shifting capital flows can significantly reshape industries, offering new opportunities and threats to your venture. To thrive in a rapidly changing world, it's crucial to understand these trends and how they impact both your current business model and your future growth strategy.

Question 1: How can AI-powered automation impact different industries? Think about efficiency gains, new product development, and changes to the workforce. What are some industries that are ALREADY being heavily disrupted?

Emerging Technologies Overview

Emerging technologies are transforming industries, creating new business models, disrupting traditional markets, and opening up entirely new opportunities for innovation.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): Machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision
  • Blockchain: Decentralized systems, cryptocurrencies, smart contracts
  • Climate Tech: Renewable energy, carbon capture, sustainable materials
  • Biotechnology: Gene editing, personalized medicine, synthetic biology

2. Impact Analysis Framework

Assignment: Choose one industry you're familiar with and analyze how emerging technologies will reshape it over the next 5-10 years. Consider:
  • Current pain points and inefficiencies
  • Which technologies could address these issues
  • New business models that might emerge
  • Potential disruption to existing players

Industry Examples

Healthcare: AI for diagnostics, telemedicine platforms, personalized treatment plans

Finance: DeFi protocols, robo-advisors, fraud detection systems

Manufacturing: IoT sensors, predictive maintenance, autonomous robots

Education: Adaptive learning platforms, VR/AR training, blockchain credentials

Question 3: What new business opportunities do you see emerging from the intersection of climate tech and AI? Consider sectors like energy, agriculture, and transportation.

Key Takeaways

  • Stay informed about technological developments in your industry
  • Identify which technologies solve real customer problems
  • Consider both opportunities and threats to your business model
  • Build partnerships with technology innovators
  • Develop a framework for evaluating emerging trends
